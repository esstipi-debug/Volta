## 7. APP TRACKING TRANSPARENCY (ATT)

### 7.1 What is IDFA?

IDFA = Identifier for Advertisers on iOS
- Allows tracking your activity in other apps
- You can allow or deny in Settings → Privacy

### 7.2 How We Use It

**If you allow:**
- Track if you download fitness-related apps
- Optimize ads for you
- Improve recommendation algorithms

**If you deny:**
- App works the same (without cross-app tracking)
- We only track within VOLTA

### 7.3 Your Control

You can change at any time:
- iOS Settings → Privacy → App Tracking → VOLTA
